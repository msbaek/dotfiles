Tool and Environment Failures Causing Workaround Cascades
You frequently hit issues where Playwright isn't available, Chrome conflicts block browser automation, or MCP servers fail to connect—leading to time-consuming fallback chains. You could preemptively check tool availability or document known environment constraints in your CLAUDE.md so Claude avoids dead-end approaches.

  • When summarizing a Medium article, Playwright was unavailable and Medium blocked direct WebFetch access, forcing Claude to fall back to web search—a multi-step degradation that could have been avoided if the tool limitation was known upfront.
Deliverable Format and Depth Mismatches
Claude's first drafts often miss your expectations on output format, level of detail, or medium (e.g., producing markdown when you need PPT, or producing a shallow draft when you want dense detail). You could mitigate this by including explicit format requirements, reference examples, or word-count targets in your initial request.
